SRIFOTON 2024

SRIFOTON (Sriwijaya Informatics Exhibition) 2024 is an annual national IT competition that provides a platform for IT students across Indonesia to develop and showcase their interests and talents in the IT field. SRIFOTON 2024 carries the theme "Digital Innovation for a Sustainable Future." SRIFOTON 2024 will host competitions, workshops, and talk shows that will cover topics such as Web Development and Personal Branding in the digital era.

Manajamen UGKMB

I created a landing page for the Management Department at Universitas Graha Karya Muara Bulian. This project provides clear and engaging information about the program, facilities, and activities for prospective students.
